ADMISSION PROCEDURE


In order to register your child for a place: Application form should be downloaded from the school website at www.essygold.com or purchased directly from the school office during any working day, the office is opened from 8am – 4pm daily. Except on public holidays.

  • First Bank: Account name: Essy Gold N/P Sch. Account number: 2029495441
  • Union Bank: Account name: Essy Gold N/P Sch. Account number: 0011963193

Duly completed Application forms are to be submitted along with

  • 2 recent passport photographs and copies of the following documents:
    • a. b. c. Immunisation record Birth certificate
    • Last school report (Previous term) and transfer certificate where applicable.
    • Receipt of payment of registration fee and originals of the above stated documents will be required for sighting.

When forms are submitted, an appointment can be booked in the admissions office to tour the school premises.

Applicants will undergo a test according to their age category.
